About me

I’m an MPhil student studying speech and language processing at the Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), supervised by Prof. Xunying Liu and Prof. Helen Meng. Before that I gained MEng and BA degrees in Information Engineering from the University of Cambridge. My current research focuses on Automatic Neurocognitive Disorder Detection.
